In an emergency there are several numbers one can call: the police department, the fire department, the central office, the local neighborhood watch. Jerry has just moved to town, and asks Fran for the best number to call in case of emergency. Fran knows that the police department is a good place to call, but she doesn't know you can call the other places. She gives Jerry the number for the police department.
